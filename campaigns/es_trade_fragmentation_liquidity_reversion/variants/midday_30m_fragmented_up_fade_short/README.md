# midday_30m_fragmented_up_fade_short

Mechanic: at 11:30, 12:30, and 13:30 ET, short ES after a completed 30-minute
up move when same-clock trade-count rank is high and same-clock average-trade
size rank is low. It enters on the next 1-minute bar open and uses per-slot
flatten times ending no later than 14:00 ET.

This expresses the campaign edge as midday fragmented-liquidity reversion after
upward pressure.
