# large20_opposite_two_sided_1400

This variant follows the sign of completed 60-minute large20 signed-flow
imbalance at 14:00 ET only when residual smaller-flow imbalance is opposite. It
enters on the next 1-minute bar open and flattens by 15:15 ET.

The mechanic is the strictest expression of the edge: larger trades must
directly disagree with residual smaller flow.
