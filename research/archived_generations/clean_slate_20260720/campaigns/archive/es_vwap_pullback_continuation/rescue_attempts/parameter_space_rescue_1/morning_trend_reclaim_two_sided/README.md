# morning_trend_reclaim_two_sided

This variant trades the same VWAP pullback-continuation edge after a short sequence of closes on the trend side of VWAP. From 09:45 through 12:00 ET, it enters after a completed pullback to VWAP reclaims the trend side.

Tunable parameters are fixed before testing: two entry parameters, percent stop, and fixed-R target.
