# morning_15m_fragmented_down_fade_long

Mechanic: at 10:00, 10:30, and 11:00 ET, long ES after a completed 15-minute
down move when same-clock trade-count rank is high and same-clock average-trade
size rank is low. It enters on the next 1-minute bar open and uses per-slot
flatten times ending no later than 11:20 ET.

This expresses the campaign edge as morning fragmented-liquidity reversion
after short-window downward pressure.
