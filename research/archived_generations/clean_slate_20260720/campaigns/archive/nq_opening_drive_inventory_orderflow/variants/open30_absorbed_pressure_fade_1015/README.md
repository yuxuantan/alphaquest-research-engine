# open30_absorbed_pressure_fade_1015

Use only the completed 09:30-09:59 ET opening window. At 10:15 ET, short after high-volume upside opening pressure if price has not extended enough to confirm continuation; flatten at 10:45 ET unless stop or target is hit.

Tunable parameters are fixed before testing: opening return threshold, opening volume-rank threshold, percent stop, and fixed-R target. NQ opening-return thresholds were chosen from unconditional opening-window distributions before staged PnL testing.
