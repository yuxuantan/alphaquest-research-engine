# large10_loose_short_1230

This variant follows negative completed 30-minute large10 signed-flow imbalance
at 12:30 ET when larger-trade pressure materially undercuts residual smaller-flow
imbalance. It enters on the next 1-minute bar open and flattens by 13:30 ET.

The mechanic tests the same trade-size information edge on the short side during
the midday session.
