# broad_up_early_signed_long_1000

Long-only ES continuation when at least four of seven sector ETFs open up, the average sector opening gap is non-negative, ES has moved at least two ticks up from the RTH open by 10:00 or 10:30 ET, and completed aggregate signed volume confirms buying pressure. This tests whether broad same-day cash-market risk-on breadth plus ES participation has enough follow-through after costs.
