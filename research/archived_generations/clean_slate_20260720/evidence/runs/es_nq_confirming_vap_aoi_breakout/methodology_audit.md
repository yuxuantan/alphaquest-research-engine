# Methodology Audit: es_nq_confirming_vap_aoi_breakout

Verdict: FAIL

Pre-test controls:
- Eight variants were declared before staged PnL testing in `campaigns/es_nq_confirming_vap_aoi_breakout/campaign.yaml`.
- The eight-variant expansion was justified before testing because the variants are distinct AOI/confirmation mechanics: all-market, combined prior/opening, 15-minute confirmation, prior extremes, opening range, overnight extremes, direct VAH/VAL, and LVN breakout.
- The pre-PnL density audit was written to `research_artifacts/es_nq_confirming_vap_aoi_breakout_density_audit_20260623.md` and excluded a ninth lower-priority horizon sibling rather than using the cap indiscriminately.
- Grid dimensions were fixed at two entry tunables, one stop tunable, and one target tunable, for 81 combinations per variant.
- Data source was the derived local ES Sierra footprint/VAP/overnight AOI cache joined to completed ES/NQ rolling lead-lag fields.
- Prior VAP fields are frozen from completed prior RTH sessions, prior RTH levels are generated by `pdh_pdl_sweep`, opening range is available only after 30 completed RTH minutes, and entries occur no earlier than the next one-minute open.
- No current-session final profile, final VWAP, future high/low, future orderflow, or post-entry data is used in the signal.

Result:
- All eight variants failed `limited_core_grid_test`.
- Every variant had 0/81 profitable iterations, zero benchmark-passing combinations, and zero Apex-rule-violating iterations.
- No variant reached monkey testing, WFA, Monte Carlo, simulated incubation, frozen validation, or candidate reporting.
- No rescue was used.

Failure interpretation:
The edge is rejected as tested. The least-bad top row was `opening_nq30_1200` with top net -900.0, PF 0.9624765478424016, MAR -0.12925029298237475, 250 trades, and failure reason `min_total_net_profit;max_consecutive_losses`. This is still negative after costs and fails the first objective rejection gate, so there is no candidate strategy from this campaign.
