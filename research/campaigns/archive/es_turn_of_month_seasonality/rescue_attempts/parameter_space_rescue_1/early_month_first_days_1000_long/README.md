# early_month_first_days_1000_long

This variant isolates the early-month side of the turn-of-month effect. It goes long only during the first configured calendar days of a month after the completed 09:55-10:00 ET 5-minute bar and flattens by 15:55 ET.

Tunable parameters are fixed before testing: first calendar days, percent stop, and fixed-R target.
