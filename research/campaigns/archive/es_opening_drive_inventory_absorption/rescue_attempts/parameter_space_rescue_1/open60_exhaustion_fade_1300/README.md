# open60_exhaustion_fade_1300

This variant shorts after a completed first-60-minute upside opening drive when price remains extended versus intraday VWAP and cumulative delta still reflects buy pressure by 13:00 ET. The signal is evaluated at the 12:59 close for intended next-bar execution at 13:00 ET and flattens at 14:00 ET.

Tunable parameters are fixed before testing: opening return threshold, opening volume-rank threshold, percent stop, and fixed-R target.
